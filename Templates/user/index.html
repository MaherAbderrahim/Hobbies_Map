About HobbiesMAP

HobbiesMAP is your go-to platform for discovering and sharing exciting activities around the world. From local events to global adventures, we make it easy to find fun experiences and share them with the community. Join us in building an interactive map of activities that everyone can contribute to!

  • Explore Activities Nearby

    Search for exciting activities based on your location and discover things to do around you.

  • Share Your Own Activities

    Contribute by adding activities to the map, so others can enjoy the experiences you love.

  • Collaborative and Verified Content

    Activities are validated by our community, ensuring that the content is accurate and trustworthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers to help you get the most out of HobbiesMAP.

How do I add a new activity to HobbiesMAP?

To add a new activity, simply log in to your account and click on the "Add Activity" button. You’ll be prompted to provide details such as the activity name, description, location, and any images. After submission, it will be reviewed and published by the admin.

How can I rate an activity?

You can rate an activity by clicking on the stars displayed on the activity page. You can give a rating from 1 to 5 stars based on your experience with the activity.

How can I report inappropriate or outdated activities?

If you find an activity that is inappropriate or outdated, you can report it by clicking the "Report" button on the activity page. The admin will review the report and take necessary actions.

Is HobbiesMAP available in all regions of Tunisia?

Yes, HobbiesMAP is available for users across Tunisia. You can explore activities in your region or discover exciting places throughout the country.

How can I connect with other users?

You can connect with other users by leaving comments and ratings on activities you’ve tried. You can also follow other users to stay updated on their posts and activities.
